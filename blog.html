Why Maruti Insurance

Eight Reasons to Choose Maruti Insurance


1. Near Cash-less Accident Repairs Pan-India
You can avail Cash-less accident repairs across India, at Maruti Suzuki’s extensive network of dealers and service stations. You do not have to pay for the whole cost of repairs. You are charged only for the compulsory excess and applicable depreciation as per the Motor Tariff. All other repairs and replacement costs are payable under the policy.

2. Instant Policy Issuance
You get your Maruti Insurance policy instantly at the time of purchase of your vehicle. You can also download the policy directly from our website without having to wait for it to arrive later. Maruti Insurance offers extremely easy ways for renewing your policy – you can renew it online, call our support centre, SMS your request, email policy details, write to us or visit the nearest dealership.

Our customer care specialists are available from the morning till night whenever you need them. Our experts can assist you in case of claim initiation, policy renewal, grievance redressal, policy changes or even for general queries.

In case of requirement, our dealers assist in towing your car to the nearest Maruti Suzuki service centre for free (up to a towing cost of Rs. 1,500).

All services related to your Maruti Insurance policy are delivered at your Doorstep. This results in considerable Saving of Time and Cost, and is Highly Convenient. In other policies, you need to do the running around or follow up with insurance companies yourself.
