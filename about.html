About Us

GEM Motors (India) Pvt Ltd., an ISO 9001- 2008 certified Company is one of the Authorized Dealers of Maruti Suzuki cars which has started Sales operations in the IT Hub of Hyderabad in September, 2007, and the Workshop operations started in the month of October, 2007.

True Value operations started in the year 2008 in Madhapur.
We primarily focus on providing better services to our customers, and as a result of the hard work we were awarded as the “Best Dealer" for the year 2008 and that too within a short period of time

In the coming years, GEM is planning to open few more showrooms across Hyderabad with a team of dedicated professionals committed to provide the best possible service to its customers.

The Foundation of GEM is based on Hard Work, Sincerity, Integrity and Commitment of its team. We nurture a culture of excellence & innovation. We are continuously striving to create a “One-Stop-Shop” for our customers.

Our aim is to become “The Preferred Destination” for purchase and service of Maruti cars, as well as for allied services (like insurance, pre-owned cars).

GEM Motors has a strong culture of excellence in everything that it does. It has helped us in earning tremendous loyalty from customers, resulting in a large percentage of its business coming through the references.
